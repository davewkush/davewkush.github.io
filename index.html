Velkommen/Welcome! I'm a linguist whose primary areas of interest are syntactic theory, sentence processing, and their intersection. I teach at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), where I also run the ØyeLab. I'm affiliated with the AcqVA research group in Norway and with Haskins Laboratories in the USA.
